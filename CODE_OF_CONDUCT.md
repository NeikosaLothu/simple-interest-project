# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation respectful and inclusive.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful
- No harassment or discrimination
- Use appropriate language

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code.

## Scope
This applies within project spaces and public interactions.

## Enforcement
Violations may result in warnings or removal.
